Founders

CalcLab started as a project to help people with math

We started CalcLab with a simple mission: to make math accessible and less intimidating for everyone. Initially, we built advanced tools to help students struggling with math concepts, providing step-by-step solutions and personalized practice problems. But as we saw how many people were still overwhelmed by the complexity of learning math on their own, our little project grew faster than we ever imagined.
